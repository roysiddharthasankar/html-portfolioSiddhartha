The Best Movies According to Siddhartha

My Top Three Movies Of All Time


1. Shawshank Redemption

I really like Andy Dufresne and I can relate to him.
He always tried to help others and create solutions to problems even though he was wrongfully charged to Prison.
This kept me to maintain a positive mentality all the time.

2. Kimi No Na Wa

It has great storyline and each and every frame is screenshot worthy,
and the characters were very wholesome.
It's a movie for hopeless romantics and this movie left me in tears even though I don't know the reason.

3. Rockstar

This movie is very close to me.
It has the best character transition I've seen ever in my life.
The protagonist, Ranbir Kapoor is an excellent actor which he proved in this film.
I've seen this movie 5-6 times and continue to do so.
